Real Analysis II (Advanced Multivariable Calculus)

Syllabus:

  • Functions of several variables, Directional derivative, Total derivative, Chain rule and mean value theorems, Taylor’s theorem.
  • Contraction mapping principle, Inverse function theorem, Implicit function theorem.
  • Extremum problems, Extremum problems with constraints, Lagrange’s multipliers method. 
  • Multiple integrals, Change of variables, Green theorems, Stokes theorems, Divergence theorem.

Suggested Books:

  1. Rudin, W. Principles of mathematical analysis, 3rd edition, McGraw-Hill Book Co., 1976.
  2. Apostol, T. M. Mathematical Analysis, Narosa, 1987.
  3. Spivak, M. Calculus on Manifolds, W.A. Benjamin, co., 1965.
