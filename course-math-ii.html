Math II

Syllabus:

  • Complex Analysis: Cauchy-Riemann equations, Analytic Functions, Harmonic functions, Elementary functions, Cauchy’s Integral Formula and its applications, Taylor series and Laurent expansions, Cauchy residue formula, Evaluation of improper integrals.
  • Probability measure on events- Definition, Properties, Examples. Conditional probability- Definition, Properties, Examples, Bayes theorem, Independent events. Definition of random variables, Standard discrete and continuous random variables -viz. Bernoulli, Binomial, Geometric, Poisson, Exponential, Normal, Expectation, Variance, Other properties.
  • Laplace transforms, Shifting theorems, Convolution theorem and its applications in ODEs, Fourier series and its applications in PDEs, D’Alembert solution to the Wave equation, Fourier Integrals, Two-Dimensional Heat equation: Solution by Fourier Series.

Suggested Books:

  • Brown, J. W. and Churchill, R. V. Complex Variables and Applications, 7th Ed., Mc-Graw Hill, 2004.
  • Kreyszig, E. Advanced Engineering Mathematics, 10th Ed., Wiley, 2015.
