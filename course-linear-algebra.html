Linear Algebra

Syllabus:

    System of linear equations, Elementary operations, row reduced echelon form, Vector spaces and subspaces with examples, Direct sum and algebra of subspaces, Row and column space of a matrix, Linear transformations, Rank-Nullity Thorem, Matrix representation of a linear operator, Change of Basis, Similarity, Dual space, Inner product spaces, Gram Schmidt Orthogonalization Process, Eigenvalues and eigenvectors of special matrices, Cayley Hamilton theorem, Diagonalization, Spectral theorem, Eigenvalues and eigenvectors for solution of Differential equations, Jordan canonical form.

Suggested Books:

    Hoffman, K. and Kunze, R., Linear Algebra, second edition, Prentice-Hall, New Delhi, 1978
