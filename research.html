Research

I specialize in the intersection of Calculus of Variations, Partial Differential Equations and application of Nonlinear Functional Analysis.

I am primarily interested in variational problems in a geometric setting, especially in problems where analysis meets geometry and topology.

Central Themes:

  • Calculus of Variations and Application of Nonlinear Functional Analysis

  • Regularity Theory for PDEs typically Elliptic and Parabolic type

  • Unique Continuation Problems for Elliptic PDEs
