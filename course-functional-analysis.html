Functional Analysis

Syllabus:

  • Normed linear spaces, Riesz lemma, Bounded linear maps, Analytic and Geometric Form of Hahn-Banach theorem and its applications, Dual of normed spaces, Reflexivity.
  • The Baire Category Theorem and its applications:- Open mapping and Closed graph theorems, Uniform boundedness principle.
  • Weak Topologies, Banach–Alaoglu–Bourbaki Theorem, Reflexive Spaces, Separable Spaces, Uniform Convexity. 
  • Hilbert Spaces, Compact Operators. Spectral Decomposition of Self-Adjoint Compact Operators.

Suggested Books:

  1. Brezis, H. Functional analysis, Sobolev spaces and partial differential equations. Universitext. Springer, New York, 2011.
  2. Bachman, G. and Narici, L. Functional Analysis. Dover Publications, 2012
  3. Lax, P. D. Functional Analysis. Pure and Applied Mathematics: A Wiley Series of Texts, Monographs and Tracts. Wiley, 2014.
  4. Rudin, W. Functional Analysis. McGraw-Hill Science/Engineering/Math, 1991.
