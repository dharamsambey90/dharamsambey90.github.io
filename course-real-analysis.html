Analysis (Math IV) — Real Analysis

Syllabus:

  • Rational numbers, Real numbers, Convergence of sequences, Bolzano- Weierstrass theorem, Convergence of series of real numbers.
  • Limit of a function, Continuity - both sequential and epsilon-delta definitions, Intermediate value theorem.
  • Differentiability, Chain Rule, Rolle's theorem, Mean value theorem, Taylor's theorem, Power series, L’Hospital’s rule, Maxima-Minima problems.
  • Riemann integration, Fundamental theorem of calculus, Improper integrals, Application of integration to summability of series.

Suggested Books:

  1. Introduction to Real Analysis, by Bartle and Sherbert.
  2. Mathematical Analysis, by Tom M. Apostol.
